ABOUT LUMOLEND

AI finds it. A human funds it.

LumoLend exists because pricing a mortgage should take minutes, and closing one should be handled by a person whose track record you can verify.

What LumoLend is

LumoLend is a mortgage marketplace operated by Honest Casa LLC (NMLS #1566096, CA DRE #02022356) — the company behind HonestCasa. The pricer runs live indicative math across home purchase, refinance, DSCR rental, short-term rental, fix & flip bridge, and HELOC scenarios, with no documents and no login. When you submit a scenario, it goes to a licensed loan officer for review — nothing is auto-approved, and pre-approval letters are issued only after a human has looked at the file.

How we make money

In accordance with RESPA, LumoLend may receive compensation from lending partners when a borrower is matched through the platform. You're never required to use a lender we suggest, and you're always free to shop. Our incentive is simple: scenarios priced honestly convert into closed loans and referrals; scenarios priced with fantasy rates don't survive underwriting — so we don't publish fantasy rates.

The library

Everything we know is public: 50+ plain-English guides covering DSCR lending state by state, self-employed documentation, bridge math, and equity strategy — plus free calculators that run the same formulas underwriters use. No email walls, ever.
